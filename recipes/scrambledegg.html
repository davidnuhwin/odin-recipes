Scrambled Egg Recipe

Home

Description

Whip up a quick and delicious breakfast with this simple scrambled egg recipe. Fluffy and light, these eggs are seasoned with a pinch of salt and pepper, then gently cooked in butter to creamy perfection. Perfect for a satisfying meal any time of the day!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • optional: 1 tablespoon milk or cream
  • fresh herbs (like chives or parsley) for garnish

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl
  2. add salt, pepper, and optional milk or cream.
  3. Whisk until well combined.
  4. Melt butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour in the eggs and let them sit for a few seconds.
  6. Gently stir and fold the eggs until they are just set and creamy.
  7. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh herbs if desired.
